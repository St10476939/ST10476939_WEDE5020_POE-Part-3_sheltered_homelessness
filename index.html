Sheltered Homelessness

Providing safety and protection

Sheltered Homelessness is a non-profit organisation dedicated to helping local communities and providing shelter to homeless people.

About Us

Founded by Rifumo Mabasa, the organization helps homeless people keep warm, access basic food, and provides safe housing.

Mission Statement

We aim to ensure safer and cleaner communities while providing protection and shelter to homeless people.

Goals & Objectives

Develop a website that allows locals to report homeless individuals for proper care, while raising awareness globally through social media, blogs, and advertisements.
